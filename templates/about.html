About Us!

The focus of our project is to collect and organize data related to books and literature. The final deliverable of the project will take the form of a web application -hosted through Digital Ocean- that will emulate the functionality of IMDB. That is to say, users of our web application will be able to access information on particular books, authors, and publishers of literature.
